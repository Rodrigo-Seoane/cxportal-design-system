## Overview

Distribution Controls gives operators a direct way to shift traffic between two AWS regions. It is the primary UI surface for failover workflows — routing all traffic to a healthy region or gradually rebalancing load after an incident.

The control exposes a single value: the percentage of traffic sent to Region A. Region B always receives the remainder (100 − A), so the two values are always complementary and sum to 100%.

---

## When to use

- Triggering a manual failover by moving 100% of traffic to a secondary region
- Gradually shifting traffic during a blue/green or canary deployment
- Rebalancing load after an incident is resolved
- Giving operators a tactile, auditable way to confirm traffic split before applying changes

---

## Interaction model

The split can be adjusted in two ways that stay in sync:

**Drag handle** — grab the vertical divider between the two bars and slide left or right. The value snaps to the nearest 10% on release.

**Input fields** — type a value directly into either region's field. Editing one field automatically updates the other. Values are committed on blur or Enter, and always snapped to 10% increments.

Arrow keys (↑ / ↓) on either input field adjust the value by ±10% per keystroke.
